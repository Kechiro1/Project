What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that may be stored on your computer or other device when you visit a website. They are generally used to make websites work, to keep track of your movements within the website, to remember your login details, and so on.

  • First party cookies are cookies that are placed by the website you are visiting, while third party cookies are placed by a website other than the one you are visiting.
  • Necessary cookies are essential for the technical operation of a website.
  • Performance cookies collect data on the performance of a website.
  • Functionality cookies enhance usability by remembering your choices (e.g. language, region, login).
  • Targeting/advertising cookies enable a website to send personalized advertising.
  • Session cookies are temporary cookies erased when you close your browser, while persistent cookies stay on your device until manually deleted.

For more information on cookies, visit www.allaboutcookies.org.
